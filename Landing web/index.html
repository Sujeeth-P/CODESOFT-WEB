Seasons


Season 1 : Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba

Read more!

Tanjiro Kamado's journey as he becomes a demon slayer to avenge his family and find a cure for his sister, Nezuko, who has been turned into a demon. Produced by studio Ufotable with stunning animation, the series captivates with its dynamic characters like the timid yet powerful Zenitsu Agatsuma and the wild Inosuke Hashibira, each facing off against formidable demons with unique abilities. The narrative explores themes of family bonds, redemption, and the complexities of morality in a world teeming with supernatural threats. Praised for its compelling storytelling, intense fight sequences, and emotional depth, "Demon Slayer" has garnered widespread acclaim and a massive fanbase, solidifying its place as one of the most influential anime of recent years.


Season 2 : Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba

Read more!

Season 2 consists of two arcs: the "Mugen Train Arc" and the "Entertainment District Arc." The "Mugen Train Arc" adapts the story from the popular movie "Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba the Movie: Mugen Train," focusing on Tanjiro, Nezuko, and their companions as they board the Mugen Train to investigate a series of disappearances. This arc delves into their battle against the powerful demon Enmu and explores the bond between Tanjiro and his comrades. Following this, the "Entertainment District Arc" takes place, continuing the journey of Tanjiro and his friends as they confront new demons in a mysterious entertainment district while seeking to uncover more about the demons' origins and powers.
